About us

By day, Revelations is a cosy coffee shop where friends meet to share coffee, cakes and light meals. At night, it is transformed into a vibey eatery.
It is the meeting place in Grahamstown for family, friends, business associates and couples – in short, a place for everyone from early morning breakfast to drinks late at night. With free Wi-Fi, Revelations is also a favourite temporary study or work venue. Revelations is truly the place “Where it all comes together. “If you’re looking for the perfect spot to host your next birthday party or event, the shaded courtyard is available for hire (catering options are also available.)
